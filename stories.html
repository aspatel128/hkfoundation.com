Our Story

A timeline of our grandparents’ values—hard work, service, and community—carried forward through the foundation.

Roots

The example they set

Our grandparents believed community isn’t just where you live—it’s what you build together. They showed up for neighbors, helped quietly, and treated service as a normal part of life.

Everyday service

Small actions that mattered

Whether it was supporting families during hard times, encouraging young people, or stepping in when someone needed help, they led with consistency—not attention.

Legacy

Why the foundation exists

The Hiraben Kodarbhai Foundation was created to carry that same spirit forward—local, practical, and focused on real needs.

Next steps

Turning values into action

Through future scholarships, fundraising, and community projects, we aim to support families and students with resources that open doors and strengthen neighborhoods.
