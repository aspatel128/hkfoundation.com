Our focus areas

Education support

We plan to support students through future scholarships, academic resources, and mentorship opportunities—helping them focus on learning instead of financial stress.

Community assistance

We aim to assist families and individuals facing short‑term challenges by supporting community initiatives and local aid efforts.

Long‑term investment

Our long‑term goal is to invest in initiatives that create lasting benefits—projects that strengthen neighborhoods, encourage stability, and promote opportunity.

Building the foundation

While our programs are still taking shape, our impact begins with intention, planning, and community involvement. Every initiative we pursue is designed to be thoughtful, transparent, and sustainable.

  • Foundation established in honor of our grandparents’ values
  • Community engagement and planning underway
  • Initial fundraising and outreach efforts beginning

How we measure impact

We are committed to accountability and transparency. As our programs launch, we plan to track our impact through:

  • Number of students supported
  • Funds raised and distributed
  • Community initiatives completed
  • Partnerships formed
